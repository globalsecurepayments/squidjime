Frequently Questions

Common answers to questions encountered in the community.

What are the objectives of this Token?

We aim to create incentivization mechanisms to bind interested parties in furthering the study of rapid language bridges between non-human intelligence and humans.

What do we hope to achieve?

By harnessing Web3.0 dynamics to build a community around the quest for interspecies communication, we want to accelerate the development of AI models that enable dialogue with other species.

Why this token launch a "Fair Launch"?

We have designed the tokenomics and access to the token to ensure no-one is unfairly allocated any tokens, and everyone has equal access.

How may I take part in and purchase this Token?

The token is available on decentralized exchanges from the moment of generation for anyone interested in getting involved.

What is market making?

To make a market means to be willing to trade a security against a counterparty by producing a firm bid to buy and offer to sell. The market making allocation creates a "shock absorber" to ameliorate massive price movements.

How will the community allocation be used?

This will fund a range of community building initiatives, including airdrops, tokens for tasks, and subsidies for projects integrating into the ecosyste,

Why could interspecies communications be important to me?

We have barely scratched the surface of our oceans and have already caused massive harm through pollution and over-fishing. By accelerating communication with species such as Cephalopods we will justify the protection of marine ecosystems, while potentially uncovering highly impactful scientific discoveries .

How may I take part in and purchase this Token?

Simply get a wallet, if you do not already have one, acquire Solana (or any token that trades against Squidji) and buy the token on exchanges. Keep an eye out for opportunities to earn the token.
